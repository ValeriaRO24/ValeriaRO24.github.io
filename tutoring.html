Tutoring Information - English Version

Hi!

I’ve been gaining teaching and tutoring experience for over six years for computer science and mathematics, both through one-on-one sessions and as a Teaching Assistant at the University of Toronto, where I’ve taught and led tutorials.

I'm currently accepting new students and offer support in the following areas:

  • Subjects: Computer Science and Mathematics
  • Computer Science: Java, JavaScript, Python — other languages may be possible; feel free to reach out for consultation. I'm flexible and happy to adapt to your needs!
  • Mathematics: High school (grades 9–12) and university-level courses including:
    • Functions
    • Advanced Functions
    • Calculus and Vectors
    • Linear Algebra
  • Experience: My teaching experience is grounded in years of private tutoring, multiple teaching assistantships, and my academic background in Computer Science and Mathematics. You can find more details on my Professional Experience page on the menu bar above.
    I have also studied how to teach Mathematics at the University of Granada.
  • Location & Format: Tutoring sessions are available both virtually (via Zoom) and in person. I’m located in Mississauga.

If you’re interested in working together or have any questions, don’t hesitate to reach out through the contact form below. I’d love to hear from you!
